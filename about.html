About

Hi! My name is Sammy and I currently work/study in the Boston and Philadelphia area. I am a student at Tufts University in the graduating class of 2026, where I major in Computer Science, and minor in finance. I also compete with the Tufts Men's Soccer Team.

I have also played soccer since almost when I could walk, now playing for my current university.
